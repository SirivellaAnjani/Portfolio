About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Anjani Sirivella and I am a data analyst, developer, and designer with over 5 years of experience working with Forbes 500 companies. I have a passion for working with data and turning it into compelling stories that communicate insights to stakeholders. A s a curious problem solver, I thrive on challenges and enjoy finding innovative solutions to complex problems.

I received a full scholarship for attending Manipal Institute of Technology, where I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Through my professional journey, I gained valuable skills in programming, data analysis, and visualization. I have since leveraged these skills to deliver impactful projects for various clients, spanning from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies.

In my portfolio, you will find a collection of my work that showcases my skills and expertise. From data analysis to web development and design, each project demonstrates my dedication to delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of my clients. Thank you for taking the time to review my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!

World Happiness Visualization

A notebook exploring the relationship between the happiness (Happiness Score) of countries against their health (Life Expectancy) and wealth (GDP per capita). Used scatter plots, geospatial graphs, and waffle charts to visualize the association.
